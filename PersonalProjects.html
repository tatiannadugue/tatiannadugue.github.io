Music

Singing, writing and playing trumpet since childhood, I find music in everything I do. My songwriting and live performances have proliferated since self-teaching the guitar in late 2018. I share with my community what songs lie in my head and heart.

You can find my music here on Soundcloud.

Poetry

I use my poetry for self-exploration and often my identity as a queer woman colors my poems brilliantly sapphic and romantic. Additionally, my identity as a musician influences my poems to be melodious in which I hope to present not just a story but a song or a tune for my reader to intrinsically hum in their head.

If you would like to support my work and other amazing poets you can purchase this poetry anthology I am in here.

Art
