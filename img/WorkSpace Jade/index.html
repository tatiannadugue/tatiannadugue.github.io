POETRY

Midnight Musings

Hi, I'm Tatianna Dugue, a freshman at Columbia University. This is a series of texts, thoughts, prose and poetry I have created over the years. Midnight Musings is a collection of poems and lines that have followed me all the way to sleep. They have been the poems I have woken up to, the lines that are painted to back of my eyelids as I fall asleep, the thoughts that shape my dreams.

Unwritten

All the unspoken thoughts we write

Erased in a frenzy by a cursor

So close to being revealed

But taken away in a second

How many things did u have to say to me

that were missed in an instance?

How many times did you stop

because I changed the tone?

Will you ever tell me those thoughts later on

when the time is right?

Or will that empty space be it's only memory?
