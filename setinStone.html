problem

How can visualize and document the presence of Confederate symbols in the United States?

solution

We created Set in Stone? to clearly contextualize and convey data insights to an audience that may not be used to engaging with data.

process

Background Research From February through March 2021 we conducted extensive background research focused on identifying and exploring data sets documenting confederate monuments. We used the SPLC Whose Heritage? Dataset, the Pew Research Center survey data, and pulled data from Ballotpedia.

Ideation We used design methodologies to storyboard and develop the initial flow of the data visualization.

Design Our design goal was to clearly contextualize and convey data insights to an audience that may not be used to engaging with data. We used bar charts and maps to prioritize readability since they effectively and clearly encode quantitative data. We accompanied all our visualizations with text in order to guide the usersâ€™ attention.

Prototyping We user tested continuously as we developed our visualizations and final narrative. , with over 30 user tests informing the final product.
